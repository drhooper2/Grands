Gma's Grands

Benny and Porter in the Snow!

I have been truly blessed. Maybe I need to add more text. Today we played out in the snow, and the boys each made a snowman. Ports' snowman was like Olof from Frozen because he had legs. Benny put lots of thought and detail into his snowman, including rock eyes, an icicle nose, my eag bags for the sides of his head, and a hat and scarf.

When we finished building the snowmen, we had a snowball fight. Of course I won. But they did gang up on me.
After the snowball fight, we had hot chocolate and whipping cream.

It was great to get outside for a while today, the boys needed it, and so did I.
